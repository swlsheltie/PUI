Melt in your mouth!
★ ★ ★

These are delicious! Unfortunately, today I had to eat my very first packet of these with a spoon! I was waiting at the door when UPS arrived and anxiously took it from his hands to try this new snack. But it is May and I'm in Tampa; I guess all day on the truck was enough to melt my entire box! What a waste! Aside from the poor delivery issue, I believe this is a perfect yummy snack for us deprived calorie-counters.
